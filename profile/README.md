# Welcome to Phy-lab at Chongqing University 👋

This organization is the open-source code hosting platform for the research group led by **Prof. Ning Wang** at **Chongqing University (CQU)**. Our research is dedicated to exploring the frontiers of **Physical AI**, driving the deep intersection of physical entities (e.g., UAVs, autonomous agents), ubiquitous signal security, and advanced artificial intelligence.

## 🔬 Core Research Areas

* **🤖 Physical & Embodied AI:** Bridging the Sim-to-Real gap. We focus on Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models and Vision-Language Navigation (VLN) to endow agents with closed-loop capabilities of perception, reasoning, and execution in complex real-world environments.
* **🛡️ Physical Layer Security:** Building the "underlying immune system" for Physical AI. We secure wireless communications at the signal and hardware levels, including Radio Frequency (RF) fingerprinting, GPS/signal spoofing detection, and high-fidelity simulation and validation of security mechanisms using Software-Defined Radio (SDR).
* **🚁 Low-Altitude Economy Networks:** Constructing secure, physics-aware intelligent communication networks for UAVs. We focus on communication link fraud detection in highly dynamic environments and explore the application of advanced algorithms like **Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs)** in complex physical-layer network modeling and endogenous security.

## 🚀 About Us
We embrace the open-source spirit. This repository hosts our experimental algorithm source code, RF/trajectory simulation datasets, and pre-trained model weights. Through open sharing, we aim to promote research reproducibility and academic exchange in the fields of Physical AI and underlying communication security.

# 欢迎来到重庆大学 Phy实验室 👋

本组织是重庆大学**王宁教授**主导的科研团队开源代码托管平台。我们的研究致力于探索**物理人工智能 (Physical AI)** 的前沿边界，推动物理世界实体（如无人机、智能体）、泛在信号安全防护与先进人工智能的深度交叉与融合。

## 🔬 核心研究方向

* **🤖 物理与具身智能 (Physical & Embodied AI):** 跨越数字与现实的鸿沟。聚焦视觉-语言-动作 (VLA) 模型与视觉语言导航 (VLN)，赋予智能体在复杂真实环境中感知、推理与执行的闭环能力。
* **🛡️ 物理层安全 (Physical Layer Security):** 构筑 Physical AI 系统的“底层免疫系统”。从信号与硬件底层保障无线通信安全，涵盖射频 (RF) 指纹特征提取、GPS与信号欺骗检测，以及基于软件无线电 (SDR) 的物理层安全机制验证与高保真仿真。
* **🚁 低空经济网络 (Low-Altitude Economy Networks):** 构建受物理规律约束的安全无人机 (UAV) 智能通信网络。重点研究高动态环境下的通信链路欺诈检测，并探索**物理信息神经网络 (PINNs)** 等前沿算法在复杂物理层网络建模与内生安全中的应用。

## 🚀 关于我们
我们拥抱开源精神。这里托管了课题组的实验算法源码、射频与轨迹仿真数据集以及预训练模型权重。我们希望通过开放共享，推动 Physical AI 与底层通信安全领域的科研成果复现与学术交流。
